Windows 10 ARM64 Optimized for Lumia 950/XL

Those of you that aren't aware, Lumia 950/XL users can install full Windows 10 onto their devices thanks to the magic from the devs at WoA-Project (https://github.com/WOA-Project)
But as expected it can sometime run slow and become a burden due to the various tasks and activities running in the background.
I started making this as a means to have a slightly more fluid experience when running Windows 10 ARM64, attempting to remove Services that aren't required or needed, Built-In Apps and stuff like that.

I do not condone Piracy, If used in any other way that with WoA launcher then you will need to purchase an Activation Key!

This has only been tested by me and possibly a couple other people. I am in need of Testers and people that can contribute to the Project.

There WILL be instabilities and issues as everybody uses Windows differently, I cannot replicate every scenario.
